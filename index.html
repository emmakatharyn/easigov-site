About EASI Gov and Erisa Administrative Services

Erisa is a Third Party Administrative company (TPA) founded by CS Hwa in 1981. We seamlessly bridge the gap between health insurance carriers and their policyholders. As a TPA, we are in charge of keeping important records of all policyholders and providing continuous support with claim settlements, hospital empanelment, and more. Our team at Erisa consists of experienced accountants, highly knowledgeable insurance consultants and representatives, as well as information technology and cybersecurity experts.

State of New Mexico Group Benefits Plan

The State of New Mexico Group Benefits Plan offers competitive, comprehensive medical, dental, vision, pharmaceutical, Employee Assistance Program, basic and supplemental life insurance, flexible spending accounts, and disability benefits to enhance the lives of SoNM employees and their qualified family members.
